Barista Job Requirement

Barista

Purpose:

Café Manager is responsible for the successful daily operations of the café. He/she hires, trains, manages, supports shift supervisors, and Baristas to ensure that the team provides consistent products, a clean working environment, and the highest level of guest experience.

Key Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Demonstrating passion and confidence for the Second Cup Brand
  • Honour guests by providing quick, friendly, and genuine service
  • Generates guest satisfaction; responds appropriately to guest concerns
  • Manages and motivates shift supervisors and baristas
  • Develops and implements café policies and procedures
  • Ensures that shift supervisors and Baristas are adhering to all Second Cup Standards
  • Responsible for hiring, firing, training, and conducting performance reviews
  • Assist Operations Manager with placing orders of supplies required for the café

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

  • Highly motivated
  • Handle multiple tasks at one time
  • Maintain a positive morale and professional attitude at all times
  • Excellent management and leadership skills
  • Have an understanding of food and beverage operations
  • Must be flexible and able to work days, evenings, and weekends when called upon
  • Detailed oriented; strong time management, prioritization, and organizational skills
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Communicates with coworkers & management to solve issues and improve quality & service
  • Ensures all baristas and shift supervisors are following cash handling procedures

Extra Benefits:

  • Salary + Daily Tip
  • International Certifications
  • Health Insurance
  • Paid Leaves

Skills:

Team Management, Customer Relation Management, Team Motivation

Job Category: Staff

Job Type: Part Time

Job Location: Lahore
